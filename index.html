The Problem With Yelp Reviews

Yelp reviews help us make important decisions about where we spend our time and money, and this information has transformed the way people think about businesses and, arguably, made us more savvy consumers. But, they aren’t always as accurate as you might expect, if taken at face value and purely based on stars alone, because frequently the reviews people leave will contradict their rating, making it difficult to know how people feel actually feel about businesses.

Reasons Reviews Aren't Always Helpful

Sometimes it’s that yelp users are angry when they write the review but when it comes time to give the restaurant a star rating they feel confined by the one to five system with no option to leave a half star, so they round up or down, severely impacting the restaurant’s overall review and skewing it one way or the other.
